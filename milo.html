A Bit About Me

I'm an 18-year-old programmer currently living at UCSC in Santa Cruz, California. (Go Slugs! Woo!)
I'm currently majoring in computer science (of course) with a focus in game design.

I've loved making games for as long as I can remember. I started (like most people) with Scratch but quickly moved on to engines like PICO-8 and P5 or just not using an engine.

I possibly love programming a bit too much, occasionally skipping sleep to finish projects I'm excited about (much to the disapproval of my mom (love you mom)).
